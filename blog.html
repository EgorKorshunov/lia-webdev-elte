Newly Found Bug That Will Ruin Your Game

April 11, 2020

A player with nickname N0Bra1n has reported one interesting bug. If Murloc uses his ultimate during a duel on an enemy with low health, and then the enemy kills Murloc and the second phase of the ultimate kills this enemy, then the game finishes. Use it until we fix it. Or better don't use.
