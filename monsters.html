This table contains the information about all bosses in Life in Arena The bosses appear every five found on a separate arena. Before the final boss the preparation time is 30 seconds more than usually.
Boss Stats Abilities

Furbolg
  • Damage: 300
  • Health: 5000
  • Attack time: 0.3
  • Armor: 30
  • Movespeed: 350
  • Stomp: stuns enemies around for 3 seconds and deals 300 damage.
  • Rain of Chaos: drops 6 waves of meteors on a target area dealing 150 with each wave to affected enemies.
  • Bash: each attack has a 32% to stun an enemy for 2 seconds.

Faceless
  • Damage: 1000
  • Health: 30000
  • Attack time: 0.5
  • Armor: 80
  • Movespeed: 320
  • Meteor: drops a meteor on a target area. The meteor stuns for 3 second, deals 500 damage and turns into a golem able to attack enemies.
  • Restoration: restores 3000 health over 10 seconds.
  • Rage: becomes magic immune for 7 seconds.

Ghoul
  • Damage: 2500
  • Health: 25000
  • Attack time: 1.3
  • Armor: 120
  • Movespeed: 300
  • Illusions: creates 2 illusions of himself for 60 seconds.
  • Silence: prevents the enemies in a target area from attacking and casting spells for 6 seconds.
  • Purge: every time the ghoul takes 2500 damage, he loses all negative effects.

Orn
  • Damage: 1200
  • Health: 50000
  • Attack time: 0.4
  • Armor: 150
  • Movespeed: 400
  • Evasion: has a 30% chance to avoid enemie's attacks.
  • Cleaving Attack: attacks deal 100% of damage to nearby enemies.
  • Bash: each attack has a 25% to stun an enemy for 2 seconds.
  • Kick: kicks an enemy back making him drop one random item.
